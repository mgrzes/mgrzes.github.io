Thank you for visiting my website. I am a Senior Lecturer in the School of Computing at the University of Kent, where I research and teach topics related to machine learning and data analysis. I have particular interest in deep learning, representation learning, reinforcement learning, and practical applications of artificial intelligence to natural language processing, computational creativity, cyber security, and social economics - among others.
